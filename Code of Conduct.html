Code of Conduct

Parent: I understand to do the following:

1. Provide my /our child with the necessary equipment.
2. Pay school fees every month / yearly.
3. Ensuring that my / our child arrives at school and is collected on time.
4. Ensuring that my / our child is wearing the correct school uniform.
5. Support the educators:

• Ensuring that my / our child understand the rules as set in our codes of conduct.
• Singing books and other data sent home every day.
• Ensuring that my / our child studies for a test / examination.
• Reinforcing basic good manners and pride on himself / herself, his/ her school work, and the school.
• Taking appropriate action when our / my child brings a misconduct slip or bad test results.
• Ensuring my / our child attends detention classes if the need arise.
• Attending meetings.
