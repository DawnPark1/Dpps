Our School

Dawn Park Primary School is located in the suburb of Dawn Park, Boksburg.
It is a former Model C school, with the current classification being Section 21, Quintile 5.
The school has a student population of 2200 learners and a staff compliment of 84, comprising of teachers, administration, grounds & facilities and security personnel. The school boasts an average pass rate over the years ranging between 98% and 100%.
In 2021, 91.5% of the our grade 7 qualified entrance to high school.
According to Principal Mr Motaung, although the demographics and socio-economic factors may have changed over the years, the academic results have remained consistently high.
The school attributes its achievements to the group of highly qualified and dedicated educators.
